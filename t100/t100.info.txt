The Pantech VEGA S5 (IM-A840S/SP) is an Android phone released by Pantech Corp. in July 2012. The device is the last device to have the brand "SKY", before the company renamed their brand "VEGA".
The device is an 5 inch phablet phone, with the almost same specifications with the "Pantech VEGA Racer2", except for the WLAN chipset and the display, and the camera.
